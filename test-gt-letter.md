# GT Letter 测试用例 & 执行结果

## Case 4: General Training Task 1 — 半正式书信

### 模拟用户输入
> 帮我批改一下这封雅思书信作文
> **题目：** You recently moved to a new apartment and have been experiencing problems with the heating system. The landlord promised to fix it but nothing has been done. Write a letter to the landlord. In your letter:
> - explain the situation
> - describe how this problem is affecting you
> - say what you will do if the problem is not fixed
>
> **作文：**
> ```
> Dear Mr. Smith,
> 
> How are you? I hope you are doing well. I am writing this letter to tell you about some problems in my new apartment.
> 
> You know that I moved to this apartment two weeks ago. The weather is getting cold and I need heating but the heating system is not working. I already told you about this problem last week and you said you will fix it, but until now nothing has been done. Every time I called you, you said the repairman will come tomorrow, but he never comes. I feel very disappointed about this situation.
> 
> This problem is really affecting my life badly. First of all, because the heating doesn't work, my apartment is very cold especially at night. I cannot sleep well and I caught a cold last week. Second, I am a student and I need to study at home. But when the room is cold I cannot concentrate on my study. My grades might be affected if this continues. Also, I have an old grandmother who visits me sometimes, and she cannot stay in such a cold room because her health is not good.
> 
> I hope you can solve this problem as soon as possible. If you cannot fix the heating in 3 days, I will have no choice but to take further actions. I know that according to the law, landlords must provide basic living conditions. If you don't fix it, I will report this to the housing department. Please don't let it come to that.
> 
> I look forward to hearing from you soon.
> 
> Yours sincerely,
> [Name]
> ```
